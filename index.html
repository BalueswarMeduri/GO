Faq

What is Golden Opportunity all about?

Golden Opportunity is a community-driven space where we help students and early-stage developers grow through DSA practice, development challenges, job/internship updates, and more.

Who can join this channel?

Anyone passionate about coding whether youre a first-year beginner or preparing for placements this is the right place to learn and grow together.

How often are coding challenges posted?

We post DSA challenges almost daily and hold contests weekly. Schedules may vary based on topic focus like Arrays, OOPs, Recursion, etc.

Is everything free?

Absolutely! The goal of Golden Opportunity is to help everyone level up, without barriers.
